About Us

Law Chambers is a new age law firm of young and dynamic legal professionals. The Firm has offices in New Delhi and Jaipur and alliances with associate lawyers in India and abroad.

Our mission is to provide outstanding legal solutions, with strong emphasis on ethics. Client Satisfaction is the cornerstone of the Firm’s work culture. Strong emphasis is placed on maintaining highest level of standards in terms of accessibility, integrity, quality and responsiveness and developing personal contacts, individual attention and strong long lasting relations.Law Chambers has therefore, carved a niche for itself as a leading Law Firm.

The Firm has successfully conducted matters involving important constitutional and commercial issues for various clients before the Supreme Court of India, State High Courts, the National Green Tribunal, National Consumer Forum, Debt Recovery Tribunal and Appellate Tribunal, Competition Commission of India, Telecom Tribunal and other tribunals. We are also conducting important Arbitration and Mediation matters. The Firm is proud to be well known in all fields of law from litigation to resolution.

The Firm is actively associated and is a member of International Bar Association (IBA), Lawasia, American Bar Association (ABA) and the Bar Association of India (BAI).
